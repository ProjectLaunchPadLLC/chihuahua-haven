Chihuahuas are small, loyal, and full of personality. This guide covers feeding, grooming, training, and health essentials to help your Chihuahua thrive.

Feeding

Chihuahuas need calorie‑dense food in small portions. Aim for ¼–½ cup per day depending on weight and activity.

Grooming

Brush short‑coat Chihuahuas weekly and long‑coat Chihuahuas 2–3 times per week.

Training

Positive reinforcement works best. Keep sessions short and fun.

Health Tips

Watch for dental issues, hypoglycemia, and joint problems. Regular vet visits are essential.
