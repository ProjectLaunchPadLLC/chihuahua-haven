Feeding Guide for Healthy Chihuahuas

Proper nutrition is essential for keeping your Chihuahua energetic and healthy. Here’s what you need to know.

Portion Sizes

Most adult Chihuahuas thrive on ¼–½ cup of high‑quality food per day, split into two meals.

Best Food Types

Look for small‑breed formulas with high protein and healthy fats. Avoid fillers like corn and soy.

Foods to Avoid

Never feed chocolate, grapes, onions, garlic, or anything containing xylitol.

Treats

Treats should make up no more than 10% of daily calories. Opt for small, soft treats suitable for tiny mouths.
